RUNNING

Running in humans is associated with improved health and life expectancy.Some of these benefits include potential weight loss, improved cardiovascular and respiratory health (reducing the risk of cardiovascular and respiratory diseases).

DUMBBELL

The dumbbell, a type of free weight, is a piece of equipment used in weight training.Weight training usually requires different types of equipment, most commonly kettle bells, dumbbells, barbells, weight plates, and weight machines.

CYCLING

The physical exercise gained from cycling is generally linked with increased health and well-being.Cycling is often use by people seeking to improve their fitness and cardiovascular health.

HEART EXERCISE
